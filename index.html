Basic Skincare Routine

1. Cleanser

Use a gentle cleanser to remove oil, dirt, and makeup without making your skin feel too dry.

2. Moisturizer

A moisturizer helps protect your skin barrier and keeps your face soft and hydrated.

3. Sunscreen

Sunscreen is important every morning, especially if you have acne scars or dark spots.

Skincare Tips

Be Consistent

Skincare takes time. Try to follow your routine for a few weeks before judging the results.

Do Not Overdo It

Using too many strong products at once can irritate your skin and make concerns worse.

Patch Test

Test new products on a small area first, especially if your skin is sensitive.
